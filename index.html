What is Siswan Challenge?

Complete your 5K in 22 mins or 10K in 45 mins and get your registration amount back.
Yes! Thats the Siswan Challenge!
PLEASE NOTE: It is absoulutely voluntary to go for this challenge, you will get a Medal and e-Certificate if you complete 5K within 1 hour 15 minutes or 10K within 2 hours 30 minutes.

What will you get?

Both runs are timed. A dedicated team will monitor each participant's time accurate to the last second.

Each participant gets a high-performance athletic tee, a finisher's medal and an electronic certificate of completion.

There will be ample hydration and energy drinks at the aid stations for every runner.

CUT OFF TIMINGS: You would be required to complete 5K within 1 hour 15 minutes or 10K within 2 hours 30 minutes to earn your e-Certificate.
